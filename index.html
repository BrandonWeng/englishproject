About

Math Tutor Finder is brought to you by a group of students at the Univeristy of Waterloo. We are Team Innovation, and we want to change how students seek assistance.

This site is specially deisgned for the students enrolled in the Faculty of Math at the University of Waterloo. We hope that you will use this service to best serve your needs and everyone around you.
